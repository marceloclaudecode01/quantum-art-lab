# Blogger Setup Guide — Quantum Art Lab

## Step 1: Create the Blog

1. Go to [blogger.com](https://www.blogger.com)
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Click **"Create Blog"**
4. Settings:
   - **Title:** Quantum Art Lab
   - **Address:** `quantumartlab.blogspot.com`
   - **Theme:** Choose a clean, minimal theme (recommended: "Contempo" or "Soho")

## Step 2: Configure Blog Settings

### Basic Settings
- **Blog description:** "Exploring the intersection of quantum computing, generative art, and blockchain. Real quantum computers. Real art. Real NFTs."
- **Privacy:** Listed on Blogger (visible to search engines)
- **HTTPS:** Enable HTTPS redirect

### Posts Settings
- **Max posts shown on main page:** 5
- **Show at most:** 7 days of posts on main page

### Comments
- Enable moderation for spam control
- Allow Google accounts only (reduces spam)

### Search preferences (SEO Critical)
- **Meta tags → Description:** "Quantum Art Lab: Where quantum computing meets generative art. Tutorials, deep-dives, and real experiments with IBM Quantum, Qiskit, and blockchain NFTs."
- **Robots.txt:** Keep default
- **Custom redirects:** Not needed initially

### How to Publish Each Post
1. Go to Blogger Dashboard → **New Post**
2. Click the **HTML view** button (pencil icon → `<>`)
3. Paste the entire content from the `.html` file
4. Switch back to **Compose view** to verify formatting
5. Add **Labels** (listed at bottom of each post file)
6. Set **Search Description** (listed at top of each post file as meta description)
7. Click **Publish**

## Step 6: Google Search Console

1. Go to [search.google.com/search-console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Add property: `quantumartlab.blogspot.com`
3. Verify via Blogger (automatic if same Google account)

5. Monitor indexing — first posts should appear in 1-3 days

## Step 7: Google Analytics

1. Go to [analytics.google.com](https://analytics.google.com)
2. Create a GA4 property for the blog
3. In Blogger: Settings → Other → Google Analytics → Paste Measurement ID (G-XXXXXXX)

## Step 8: Apply for Google AdSense

### Requirements (apply after 3+ months):
- **Minimum 30 posts** (you'll have 10 initially — keep writing)
- **Consistent traffic** (aim for 100+ page views/day)
- **Original content** (all posts are original)
- **About page** with author info
- **Privacy Policy page** (required by AdSense)
- **Contact page**

### Required Pages to Create Before Applying:
1. **About** — "Quantum Art Lab is a technical blog exploring the intersection of quantum computing and generative art. Created by [your name], a developer who used real quantum computers (IBM Quantum, Origin Quantum) to generate 100 unique NFTs."
2. **Privacy Policy** — Use a generator like [privacypolicygenerator.info](https://www.privacypolicygenerator.info/) — mention Google AdSense, cookies, analytics
3. **Contact** — Simple form or email address
4. **Disclaimer** — "This blog contains affiliate links. NFT investments carry risk."

### How to Apply:
1. Go to [adsense.google.com](https://www.google.com/adsense)
2. Sign up with same Google account
3. Connect your blog URL
4. Add the AdSense code snippet to Blogger: Theme → Edit HTML → paste in `<head>`
5. Wait for review (usually 1-2 weeks)
